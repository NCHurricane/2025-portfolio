About Me

Cats, Clouds, Cars, and Code

I'm Chuck Copeland, a multimedia content specialist with over 20 years of freelance experience in video editing, motion graphics, web design, and content creation.

For 30 years, I have led successful teams at companies like Walmart and Harbor Freight Tools. My passion for storytelling, web design, and video editing led me to build a second, freelance career creating content for my social media accounts, local businesses, and civic organizations.

I run several niche social media accounts - focusing on topics from hurricane awareness to our feline friends to motorsports. My greatest days are when I am able to help someone, make them smile, or give them a laugh.
